Welcome to About Me!

A look into who I am.

Hey, I'm Stevan Likusic!

I'm a game programmer and developer originally from Serbia, now based in Stockholm, Sweden. Ever since I was a kid, I've been passionate about games, mostly immersing myself in competitive multiplayer experiences while also cherishing single-player adventures with great stories.

Currently in my final year at Futuregames, I specialize in systems and logic but love exploring all aspects of game development. I'm curious, eager to learn, and always looking for new challenges!

Outside of development, I enjoy playing games, listening to music, and spending quality time with my friends and family. I also love traveling, discovering new activities and hoobies, and unwinding with a great book, comic, or show!

Strengths & Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Problem-solving - I enjoy breaking down complex challenges and finding efficient solutions.
  • Quick learner - I adapt fast and pick up new tools, concepts, and workflows with ease.
  • Open-minded & easy-going - I thrive in team environments and enjoy learning from different perspectives. I really value collaboration!
  • Research-driven - I love diving deep into topics I'm passionate about, ensuring I fully understand and apply what I learn.
  • Creative thinker - I tend to approach problems from different angles and enjoy experimenting with new ideas.

Weaknesses

  • Perfectionism - I tend to refine details more than necessary, sometimes spending too much time on minor aspects.
  • Taking on too much - I often juggle multiple projects, which makes prioritization challenging.
  • Self-critical - I set high expectations for myself, which sometimes slows me down.
  • Prefers structure - I work best with clear goals and deadlines. It keeps me in line with my responsibilities.
  • Overthinks solutions - I sometimes spend too long analyzing different approaches instead of quickly iterating.
