About Me

Hi, I'm Stevan Likusic, a 21-year-old game programmer and developer originally from Serbia, now based in Stockholm, Sweden. Ever since I was a kid, I've been passionate about games, mostly immersing myself in big competitive multiplayer experiences while also cherishing the moments of peace that come with single-player games with great stories.

As a student in my final year at Futuregames, I have dedicated myself to improving my skills and deepening my knowledge as a game developer. I specialize mostly in systems and logic, but I enjoy exploring all aspects of game development as I'm quite curious about everything and I love learning new things!

Beyond game development, I also enjoy playing games, listening to music, and spending quality time with friends.
